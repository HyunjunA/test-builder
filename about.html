Our Mission

App Collection is a curated set of free, easy-to-use online tools designed to bring fun and utility to your daily life. We believe that powerful tools should be accessible to everyone, without complicated sign-ups or hidden fees. Every tool we build runs entirely in your browser, ensuring fast performance and complete privacy.

How It Works

All our tools are built with modern web technologies and run directly in your browser:

  • Lotto Number Generator uses a cryptographically-aware random number algorithm to produce fair, unbiased lottery number sets. You can customize the maximum number and how many numbers to draw, and instantly generate 10 unique sets. The interface supports 14 languages including English, Korean, Japanese, Chinese, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Russian, Arabic, Hindi, Thai, Vietnamese, and Italian.
  • Dog vs Cat AI Classifier is powered by TensorFlow.js and Google's Teachable Machine. The machine learning model runs entirely on your device, meaning your photos are never uploaded to any server. You can use your webcam for live classification or upload a photo for instant analysis with confidence percentages.

Technology

Our platform is built using vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript with no heavy frameworks, ensuring lightning-fast load times and broad browser compatibility. We leverage the following technologies:

  • TensorFlow.js for client-side machine learning inference
  • Teachable Machine for pre-trained image classification models
  • Responsive Design with CSS Grid and Flexbox for optimal viewing on any device
  • Progressive Enhancement ensuring core functionality works across all modern browsers

Privacy First

We take your privacy seriously. Our AI classifier processes all images locally on your device and never transmits them to external servers. We use minimal cookies only for essential features like theme preference and language selection. For full details, please review our Privacy Policy.
